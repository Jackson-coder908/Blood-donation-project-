Why It Matters

Blood Cannot Be
Manufactured

Only generous donors like you can meet the need. Here's the impact your donation makes.

🏥

Trauma & Surgery

Accident victims and surgical patients can require up to 100 units of blood. Donors make emergency care possible.

👶

Newborn Care

Premature babies often need small, frequent transfusions. Your donation can nurture the very beginning of a life.

♋

Cancer Patients

Chemotherapy destroys blood cells. Cancer patients may need transfusions every few days throughout treatment.

🧬

Chronic Illness

Patients with sickle cell, thalassemia, and other conditions rely on regular transfusions to survive.

💪

Good for You Too

Donating blood burns calories, reveals health insights, and has been linked to reduced heart disease risk.

🤝

Community Impact

Local blood banks serve local hospitals. Your donation stays in your community, saving your neighbors.

Am I Eligible?

Basic Requirements

Most healthy adults can donate. Check the basics below.

✓ You Can Donate If...

  • You are 17 years or older (16 with parental consent in some states)
  • You weigh at least 110 lbs (50 kg)
  • You are in good general health
  • You have not donated whole blood in the last 56 days
  • You have had adequate sleep and eaten a meal today

✗ Temporary Deferrals

  • Cold, flu, or active infection
  • Recent tattoo or piercing (within 3 months)
  • Travel to certain countries (varies)
  • Pregnancy or recent childbirth
  • Some medications (ask our staff)

Not sure? Our staff will walk you through it.
